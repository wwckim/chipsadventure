Our friend Chip is on an excellent adventure! Chip has been so inspired by Conrad Hilton’s vision to, “fill the earth with the light and warmth of hospitality,” that he packed his bags and went on a Doubletree tour. Chip realizes the benefits his kind can bring to humans and decides that he needs to spread the light and warmth of hospitality not just to the earth, but to SPACE. Rejuvenated with the idea of helping homesick astronauts feel closer to the Earth, Chip is on his most excellent adventure yet.
